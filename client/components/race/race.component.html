Liberty Pines Academy's inaugural RUNNING OF THE WOLVES 5K and 1 mile fun run will be held Sunday, October 16th at St. Johns Golf and Country Club. All proceeds from the run will benefit Liberty Pines Academy, a St. Johns county K-8 A rated school with over 1400 students. The run is open to the public. It is a flat, fast, two turn lollipop course along the community's scenic roads that finishes on the grass at the athletic field.
